Benji Mobile App

Benji is a retail mobile app and institutional web app that allows investors to browse tokenized securities and cryptocurrencies and invest in the world’s first tokenized money fund that is natively issued on blockchains. Benji makes it easy to securely buy, sell, and view your account activities through the apps.

Benji is offered and operated by Franklin Distributors, LLC, part of the Franklin Templeton global asset management organization.
